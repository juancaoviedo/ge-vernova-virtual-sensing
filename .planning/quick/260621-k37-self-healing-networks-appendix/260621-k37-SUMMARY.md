# Phase quick-260621-k37 Plan 01: Self-Healing Distribution Networks Appendix Summary

Authored a standalone, self-contained 11-card HTML study appendix on **self-healing /
FLISR** — the action layer on top of virtual sensing — driven by two source PDFs, and wired

## What Was Built

A new patents appendix, `appendix-self-healing-networks.md/.html`, in the same ORACS/AGMS
"card" house style as the two existing appendixes but **standalone** (light see-also only).
Content is PDF-driven from:

- **Arefifar, Alam & Hamadi (2023)**, *J. Modern Power Systems & Clean Energy* — the
  taxonomy spine (self-healing-vs-resilience, four-stage FLISR loop, five control actions,
  eight objective functions, the optimization-algorithm zoo, centralized-vs-distributed
  architectures, the open frontier).
- **Meteab, Tousi & Omran (2025)**, *Scientific Reports* — the concrete worked framework
  (IEEE 33-bus, BW/FW sweep + PSO DG placement, a Sect.-8 three-phase fault isolated by
  Sw-8/Sw-9 and restored through tie Sw-35 in ~60 ms with a 76% loss cut, permanent/temporary
  recloser branch, island-mode microgrid restoration).

**The 11 cards:** Purpose & framing · Self-healing vs. resilience · FLISR four-stage loop ·
the five control actions · the IEEE 33-bus worked run · permanent-vs-temporary fault branch ·
networked microgrids / island mode · centralized vs. distributed · objective functions &
algorithms · the open frontier · standalone wrap-up. Each opens with house-style framing,
uses comparison/spec tables where the papers earn them, amber `> blockquote` anchors, and
"the trap is…" interview-speakable framings. Role ties are light: FLISR as the ORACS
operation loop in execution, the simulate-before-commit gate = granted Operation Loop
Formation patent (US 12,596,341 B2), island-mode = the operating cell.
