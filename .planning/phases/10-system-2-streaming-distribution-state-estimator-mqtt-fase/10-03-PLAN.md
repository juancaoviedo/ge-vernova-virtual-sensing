<objective>
Implement the three estimators behind one interface and the FASE predict step. `estimators.py` provides `BaseEstimator` (abstract predict/update), `WLSEstimator` (per-snapshot Gauss-Newton with χ²/LNR bad-data and explicit rank-deficiency reporting), `EKFEstimator` (Joseph-form recursive update), and `UKFEstimator` (square-root, no Jacobian). `fase_predict.py` provides `FASEPredictor` (profile-as-noisy-forecast mean + process noise `Q`) and a random-walk persistence foil behind the same predict interface.

Purpose: The estimator family is the contrast that IS the interview narrative (WLS baseline → recursive FASE EKF/UKF; forecast-beats-persistence). Pure compute — depends only on `ac_model.py` (Plan 02) for h/H/S; no MQTT, no InfluxDB, no oracle.
Output: `estimators.py` + `fase_predict.py`, unit-verifiable on synthetic data.
</objective>
