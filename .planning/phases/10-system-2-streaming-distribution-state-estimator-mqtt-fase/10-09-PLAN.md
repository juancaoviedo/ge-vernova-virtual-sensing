<objective>
Implement **D-09**: an external `forecast` publisher over MQTT. Currently the FASE predict tried
to fabricate a forecast in-estimator from a single scalar `load_pu` — non-functional and not a
real per-bus schedule. This plan adds `forecast.py` (a sibling of `publish.py`) that reconstructs
the SCHEDULED per-bus injections from LEGAL side-information only — the static per-bus nominal
`(P_nom, Q_nom)` + `sgen`/DER placements from `build_enhanced_33bus()`, scaled by the `profiles`
multipliers `load_pu`/`solar_pu`/`wind_pu` — degrades them with seeded per-class noise, and
publishes the full per-bus injection forecast per timestep on `ieee33/{experiment}/{scenario}/forecast`.

It MUST be built from the SCHEDULE, NEVER the realized `state`/`fault_event` oracle
(oracle separation stays grep-checkable). The estimator consumes this stream in 10-10.

Depends on 10-07 (64-state / buses 1..32 convention) for the bus indexing of the payload.
Independent of 10-08 (no shared files) — runs in the same wave.

Purpose: give the FASE predict a real, honestly-imperfect per-bus forecast (R6/R7 predict input).
Output: a new `forecast` runner + `[project.scripts]` entry + estimate_config forecast knobs.
</objective>
