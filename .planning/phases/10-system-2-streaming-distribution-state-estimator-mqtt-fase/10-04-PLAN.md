<objective>
Build the consumer half of the stream. `estimate.py` is the runner that subscribes to the MQTT measurement topics and the retained `ieee33/netmodel/current`, assembles each snapshot's `z` vector and `R = diag(assumed_sigma┬▓)`, tracks the topology config (rebuilding `Ybus` via `ac_model` on every `config_version` bump and flagging the step as a known topology change), reads the `profiles` forecast schedule at startup, drives the selected estimator (`--estimator wls|ekf|ukf`, ONE per invocation) through predict (FASE) + update-on-arrival, and writes per-bus `(x╠é, P)` + `trace_P` to a new `estimates` bucket. For the recursive filters (EKF/UKF) it ALSO persists the faithful per-step innovation NIS scalar (`nis_k = y_kßĄĆ S_kŌü╗┬╣ y_k`) and innovation dimension (`m_k`) on the system-level estimate point, so the R11 NIS calibration gate (Plan 05) reads a true innovation sequence rather than a diagonal-sigma proxy. An additive `influx.write_estimate_step` helper handles persistence.

Purpose: This wires Plans 01ŌĆō03 into the live FASE estimator and exercises both regimes (day observability, fault island-mode covariance inflation). It is the SOLE place the streaming pieces meet ŌĆö and it must honor the oracle-separation boundary (D-06): measurements + netmodel + profiles only. It is also the faithful source of the NIS innovation statistics the acceptance gate depends on.
Output: `uv run estimate` produces an `estimates` bucket for any (scenario, source, estimator), with per-step `nis_k`/`m_k` for recursive filters.
</objective>
