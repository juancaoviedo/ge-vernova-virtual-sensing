<objective>
Build the consumer half of the stream. `estimate.py` is the runner that subscribes to the MQTT measurement topics and the retained `ieee33/netmodel/current`, assembles each snapshot's `z` vector and `R = diag(assumed_sigma²)`, tracks the topology config (rebuilding `Ybus` via `ac_model` on every `config_version` bump and flagging the step as a known topology change), reads the `profiles` forecast schedule at startup, drives the selected estimator (`--estimator wls|ekf|ukf`, ONE per invocation) through predict (FASE) + update-on-arrival, and writes per-bus `(x̂, P)` + `trace_P` to a new `estimates` bucket. An additive `influx.write_estimate_step` helper handles persistence.

Purpose: This wires Plans 01–03 into the live FASE estimator and exercises both regimes (day observability, fault island-mode covariance inflation). It is the SOLE place the streaming pieces meet — and it must honor the oracle-separation boundary (D-06): measurements + netmodel + profiles only.
Output: `uv run estimate` produces an `estimates` bucket for any (scenario, source, estimator).
</objective>
