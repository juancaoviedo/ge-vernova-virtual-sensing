# Recommendations to Executives

## Adopting IntelliGrid Architecture as the Strategic Vision

* **Adopt
  the IntelliGrid Architecture** as the strategic vision for your information
  infrastructure
* **Ensure
  that the different users of the IntelliGrid Architecture understand** how to
  utilize the relevant parts of IntelliGrid Architecture products, including the power system
  functional descriptions and IntelliGrid Architecture high level concepts.
* **Develop
  a plan** for implementing the recommended architecture and standards-based
  technologies, based on your specific business needs and the timeframe
  appropriate for meeting those needs and your financial constraints.
* **Provide
  feedback** so that the IntelliGrid Architecture can evolve to meet future needs and
  recommend standards that are created in the future.
