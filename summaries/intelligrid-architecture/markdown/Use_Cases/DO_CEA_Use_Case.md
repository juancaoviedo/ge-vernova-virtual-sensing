## Narrative

### Coordination of Emergency Actions

This
application recognizes the emergency situation based on changes of the
operating conditions or on reaction of some RAS to operational changes
and coordinates the objectives, modes of operation, and constraints of
other ADA applications. For example, Under-frequency Load Shedding
Schemes trigger emergency load reduction mode of volt/var control, or
the under-frequency protection of DER triggers the pre-armed
intentional islanding.
