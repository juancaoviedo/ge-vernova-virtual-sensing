### Wireless Digital Subscriber Line (WDSL)

**URL:** **http://www.dslforum.org/**

Wireless Digital
Subscriber Line (WDSL) leaps past the last mile stranglehold from the local
telephone company with leading edge wireless technology. WDSL high speed Internet
service is delivered to users via radio spectrum - utilizing fixed wireless
technologies such as MMDS or 802.11. Hence, unlike other forms of DSL, WDSL is
a shared medium. Downstream speeds typically start at 384kbps-1.5M and upstream
at 128kbps.

**Keywords:**  Modem, serial, high speed, physical layer, Access Technology,
wireless
