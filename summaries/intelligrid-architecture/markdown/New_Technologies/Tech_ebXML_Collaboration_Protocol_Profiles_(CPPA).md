### ebXML Collaboration Protocol Profiles (CPPA)

**URL:**http://www.oasis-open.org/committees/tc\_home.php?wg\_abbrev=ebxml-cppa

Collaboration Protocol Profiles (CPPs) and
Collaboration Protocol Agreements (CPAs), define a business partner's technical
capabilities to engage in electronic business collaborations with other
partners, and the technical agreement between two (or more) partners to engage
in electronic business collaboration.

**Keywords:** Enterprise VP, Limited usage
