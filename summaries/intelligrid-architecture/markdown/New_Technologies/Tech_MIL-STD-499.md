### MIL-STD-499

**URL:** http://www.incose.org/stc/mil499.htm

This
standard was published on July 17,
1969. The purpose of this standard is to provide a set of criteria
that will serve as a guide to (a) contractors preparing proposed Systems
Engineering Management Plans (SEMPs) for the conduct and management of their
systems engineering effort on a particular program; and (b) Government
personnel when either tailoring a bid work statement calling for SEMPs or
competitively evaluating and validating proposed SEMPs and negotiating them
into contract statements of work. It also provides the basis for validating the
contractor's SEM capability.
