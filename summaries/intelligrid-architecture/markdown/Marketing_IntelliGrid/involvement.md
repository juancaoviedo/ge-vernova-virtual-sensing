To Get Involved

The IntelliGrid Project will ultimately impact anyone who builds,
maintains and manages energy systems, as well as those that regulate,
manufacture products for, utilize, or otherwise consume energy from the future
energy system. This list a appears to
encompass nearly everyone on the planet. This should not be a surprise given
the dependence of society on electric power and the pervasive nature of electric
energy. Indeed, the breadth of this project spans the universe of energy and
information technologies.

Public involvement is critical to the overall
success of the IntelliGrid Architecture. The IntelliGrid Architecture team wants your input into the development of
the architecture. This is important not only to help ensure the architecture
incorporates the numerous requirements of all aspects of the power industry -
utilities, customers, government - but also to facilitate the adoption of the
architecture itself.

The IntelliGrid Project team has a number of needs to ensure the
architecture will become a reality:

* Descriptions of the communications technologies currently available for use in the industry.
* Descriptions of existing and planned utility activities and
  best practices, especially as they affect data communications
  requirements.
* Use cases describing important utility activities, especially
  new practices and technologies envisioned by leaders in the
  industry.
* Recommendations of best-practice existing technologies, when
  and where they should be used, and migration plans for utilities
  to make use of them
* Recommendations based on the analysis of new technologies
  that must be developed or modified from existing capabilities.
* Input the steps required to put IntelliGrid Architecture in place,
  especially new open standards that must be developed or
  modified.

To get involved, send [e-mail](mailto:iecsa-info@www.iecsa.org)
to the IntelliGrid Architecture team or [register](https://www.iecsa.org/twiki/pub-bin/view/Help/TWikiRegistration)
to enter the project collaboration site.
